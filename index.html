Coulee Playland is a recreational campground on the shore of Banks Lake. Camping, boat rentals, and camp store with the most extensive tackle selection in Eastern Washington.

Camping

Relax and unwind on Banks Lake. We have camping spots for tents, RV/Campers, and everything in between. Pull through sites available with an assortment of full hookups, 30amp, 50amp, and water/electric sites. Yurt and glamping options available.

Boat Rentals

We have pontoon boats, fishing boats, kayaks, pedal boats, and paddle boards for rent. As low as $15 an hour. No boaters license is required with our boat rentals. Gas charge and taxes are additional to the rental fee. Marine fuel available on the water. Call us for a reservation.

Bait Shop

We have specialty bait and tackle for many types of fishing including bass, trout, and walleye. If it swims in our local lakes, we have what you need to catch it and can give tips and tricks. Live bait available.
